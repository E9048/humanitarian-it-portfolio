CPR — Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation
الإنعاش القلبي الرئوي

For adults who are unresponsive and not breathing normally. Call 123 FIRST.

1
Check Scene
Is it safe? Tap shoulders. "Are you OK?"
→
2
Call 123
Send someone to call. Tell others to find AED.
→
3
30 Compressions
Hands on chest. Push down 5–6cm. Fast and hard.
→
4
2 Breaths
Tilt head, lift chin, give 2 rescue breaths (1 sec each).
→
5
Repeat
Continue 30:2 cycles until help arrives or AED is ready.
100
Compressions per minute
100–120 BPM
🎵
Sing mentally: "Stayin' Alive" (Bee Gees)
It's exactly the right rhythm for chest compressions

Common Emergency Situations
حالات الطوارئ الشائعة
🩸
Severe Bleeding
نزيف حاد
1
Put on gloves if available — protect yourself
2
Apply firm pressure with clean cloth — don't remove
3
Add more cloth on top if blood soaks through
4
Elevate the wounded area above heart level
5
Call 123 if severe or doesn't stop in 10 min
❌ Don't: Remove the cloth — it disturbs the clot
🔥
Burns
الحروق
1
Remove from heat source safely
2
Cool under running water for 20 minutes
3
Cover with clean non-fluffy material
4
Remove jewelry near the burn (before swelling)
❌ Don't: Use ice, butter, toothpaste, or creams
⚠️ Seek hospital if: Face/hands/large area or blisters form
😮
Choking (Adult)
الاختناق
1
Ask: "Are you choking?" — if can't speak/cough, act
2
Give 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades
3
5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver)
4
Alternate: 5 back blows + 5 abdominal thrusts
5
If unconscious: start CPR, call 123
✅ If alone and choking: thrust abdomen onto hard chair edge
🌡️
Heat Stroke
ضربة الشمس
1
Move to cool/shaded area immediately
2
Remove excess clothing
3
Apply cool (not ice cold) water to skin
4
Fan the person to encourage cooling
5
Give cool water if conscious and able to swallow
⚠️ Call 123 if: confused, unconscious, or temp above 40°C
💊
Suspected Poisoning
تسمم مشتبه به
1
Call Poison Control: 08008880700 immediately
2
Note what was taken, amount, and time
3
Keep the container/packaging for doctors
4
If unconscious and breathing: recovery position
❌ Don't: Induce vomiting unless told by medical staff
😵
Seizure
النوبة التشنجية
1
Stay calm — protect the person from injury
2
Clear the area of hard/sharp objects
3
Place something soft under their head
4
Time the seizure — call 123 if over 5 minutes
5
After: recovery position, stay until fully alert
❌ Don't: Hold them down or put anything in their mouth
🛌
Recovery Position | وضع الإفاقة
للشخص الفاقد الوعي والمتنفس

Use for unconscious persons who are breathing. Prevents choking on vomit.

1️⃣
Kneel beside them
Straighten legs, arm nearest you at right angle
2️⃣
Bring far hand to cheek
Hold hand against cheek, palm outward
3️⃣
Bend far knee
Pull far knee up while keeping foot flat
4️⃣
Roll gently
Roll toward you. Tilt head back to open airway
5️⃣
Monitor breathing
Stay with them. Call 123. Check every 10 min
