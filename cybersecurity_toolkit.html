⚠️ Common Digital Threats | التهديدات الرقمية الشائعة
Know your enemy — understand the most common threats facing individuals and organizations
🎣
Phishing
التصيد الاحتيالي
Fake emails or messages pretending to be from trusted sources (banks, government) to steal your login credentials or personal data.
CRITICAL
🔒
Ransomware
برامج الفدية
Malicious software that encrypts your files and demands payment to restore access. Common in NGO and healthcare environments.
CRITICAL
👤
Social Engineering
الهندسة الاجتماعية
Manipulating people into revealing confidential information through psychological tricks, false urgency, or impersonation.
HIGH
📡
Public Wi-Fi Attacks
هجمات الواي فاي العام
Attackers intercept your data on unsecured networks. "Man-in-the-Middle" attacks can steal passwords and sensitive info.
HIGH
🔑
Weak Passwords
كلمات المرور الضعيفة
Simple, reused, or guessable passwords are the #1 entry point for attackers. Use unique strong passwords for every account.
MEDIUM
📱
Fake Apps & Malware
التطبيقات المزيفة
Malicious apps disguised as legitimate ones. They may steal contacts, messages, or track your location without consent.
MEDIUM

Password Best Practices | أفضل الممارسات

Follow these rules for unbreakable passwords

📏
Length Wins
Longer = stronger. A 16-char passphrase beats a complex 8-char password every time.
🎲
Use Passphrases
4 random words together are memorable AND secure. Example: "Apple-Sky-Mountain-7"
🔂
Never Reuse
One breach exposes ALL accounts if you reuse. Use a password manager instead.
📱
Enable 2FA
Two-factor authentication blocks 99% of automated attacks even if your password leaks.
🎣 Phishing Detection Quiz | اختبار كشف التصيد
Can you spot the fake? Examine each email and decide: Legitimate or Phishing?
Email #1 — Spot the red flags
From: support@banq-misr.secure-login.xyz
Subject: ⚠️ URGENT: Your account will be suspended in 24 hours
To: customer@email.com
Dear Valued Customer,

We have detected suspicious activity in your account. To avoid suspension, please click here immediately to verify your identity: http://banq-misr.verify-now.info/login

Failure to act within 24 HOURS will result in permanent suspension.

Regards,
Bank Misr Security Team
Email #2 — Is this real?
From: no-reply@google.com
Subject: Security alert for your Google Account
To: yourname@gmail.com

We noticed a new sign-in to your Google Account from a Windows device in Cairo, Egypt.

If this was you, you don't need to do anything.
If this wasn't you, check your account activity at myaccount.google.com

The Google Account Team
💡 Quick Security Tips | نصائح أمنية سريعة
Share these with your community — print-friendly reference card
🔐
Strong Passwords
12+ chars, mix upper/lower/numbers/symbols. Use a password manager like Bitwarden (free).
📲
Enable 2FA
Always turn on two-factor authentication for email, social media, and banking apps.
🔄
Update Software
Keep your OS, apps, and antivirus updated. Most attacks exploit unpatched vulnerabilities.
📧
Verify Senders
Check email addresses carefully. Hover over links before clicking. When in doubt, don't click.
📡
Avoid Public Wi-Fi
Never do banking or access sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi. Use mobile data or VPN instead.
💾
Backup Regularly
Follow the 3-2-1 rule: 3 copies, 2 different media, 1 offsite (cloud). Do it weekly.
🔒
Lock Your Screen
Always lock your screen when away from your device. Use PIN, fingerprint, or face lock.
📱
App Permissions
Regularly review app permissions. Does that flashlight app really need your contacts and location?
🧠
Think Before Sharing
Personal data (ID, phone, address) shared online can be used for identity theft or scams.
